Serves 8

1/2 lb. Chaurice (hot sausage)
1 lb. Andoulle
1/4 cup tasso
1 large onion, chopped
1 bell pepper, chopped
2 stalks celery, chopped
4 cloves minced garlic
1 1-lb can tomatoes (with liquid & crushed)
2 cups Rice
3 cups chicken stock
2 cups smoked ham (diced)
4 green onions (sliced)
1/4 cup parsley (chopped)
1/2 tsp Thyme
3 bay leaves
salt (to taste)
pepper (to taste)
tabasco (to taste)

Saute hot sausage and tasso, in oil if necessary, for several minutes.  In same oil add onions, cook thoroly then add bell peppers and celery

Add rice and stir until coated in the oil.  Add tomatoes, chicken stock, garlick, sliced andouille, and ham
bring mixture to a boil.  Cover and let simmar about 30 minutes or until rice is done
before serving add green onions and parsley.  Serve with French bread and a green salad

Note:  If usking Uncle Ben's rice, then use 2 cups rice, 2 cups stock and 1 16 oz can tomatoes.
