1 eggplant, cut in finger-long strips
2 eggs
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup flour
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp white pepper
italian bread crumbs
oil for frying
tabasco

Salt eggplant fingers and let sit in colander for 20 minutes.  Rince and dry well.  Dip fingers into flour.  then dip into mixture of the eggs, milk and tabasco.

dip into bread crumbs
deep-fry slowly on both sides

you'll want to dip these in remoulade, or similar spicy sauce
