1 cup sugar
2 tablespoons baking cocoa
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup milk
1 cup quick-cook oats
1/4 cup flaked coconut
2 tablespoons peanut butter (i never put this in)
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

in saucepan, combine sugar and cocoa, add butter and milk.
Cook and stir over medium heat until mixture comes to a boil.
boil for 1 minute.
Remove from heat, stir in oats, coconut, peanut butter and vanilla.
Drop by tablespoons onto waxed paper, let stand until set.
